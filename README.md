## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Jadavpur University, currently focusing on full-stack architectures and competitive programming.

* **Competitive Programming:** I actively compete in ICPC, Codeforces, and various hackathons to continuously sharpen my algorithmic thinking and optimize complex data structures.
* **Full-Stack & Systems Engineering:** I specialize in building scalable, end-to-end architectures. My recent work focuses heavily on designing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines and integrating LLM-powered intelligence into production-ready systems.
* **Current Explorations:** I am currently deepening my technical expertise in vector databases, semantic search algorithms.
* **Let's Connect:** I am always open to discussing technology, open-source collaborations, or new opportunities. Feel free to reach out via [Email](mailto:ritamghosh548@gmail.com) or connect with me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ritamghosh548/).
